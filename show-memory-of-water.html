 What is the play all about?

Sit back, relax and enjoy peeking into the ways that three charming, acid-tongued and zany sisters manage relationships in a time of crisis and uncertainty. Secrets and lies unfold as they meet up at their late motherâ€™s home by the sea for her funeral.

The Memory of Water is a beautifully observed, award-winning comical play by Shelagh Stephenson (Laurence Olivier Award for Best New Comedy 2000) with writing that can take you from sublime family reunion to comic chaos in one swoop.
