 About Grimms' Fairy Tales

Grimms' Fairy Tales is a German collection of fairy tales by the Grimm brothers, first published on 20 December 1812. Their best known tales include “Hansel and Gretel", “Snow White,” “Little Red Riding Hood,” “Sleeping Beauty,” “Tom Thumb,” “Rapunzel,” “The Golden Goose,” and “Rumpelstiltskin”.
